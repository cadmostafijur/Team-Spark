Explore the Power of the Sun

The Sun is about 4.5 billion years old and is the heart of our solar system. It provides light, heat, and energy that sustains life on Earth.\n It is a massive, nearly perfect sphere of hot plasma, heated to incandescence by nuclear fusion reactions in its core, radiating the energy from its surface mainly as visible light and infrared radiation with 10% at ultraviolet energies. It is by far the most important source of energy for life on Earth. \n Join us on a journey to learn more about our closest star.The Sun is a star at the center of the Solar System.
